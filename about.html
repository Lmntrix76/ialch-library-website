About Our Library

The intellectual heart of Inkosi Albert Luthuli Central Hospital

Our History

IALCH Library, formerly known as Smith & Nephew Medical Library, was officially opened in July 2005. Since then, we've grown to become a vital resource for healthcare professionals in KwaZulu-Natal.

Our mission is to provide access to information and research materials for all Department of Health staff, supporting evidence-based practice and continuous learning.
