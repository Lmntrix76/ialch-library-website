Frequently Asked Questions

All full-time IALCH staff are automatically eligible for library services. To activate your account, please visit the library with your staff ID to receive your unique barcode number.

Full-time staff members may borrow up to four items at a time. The loan period is 14 working days for most materials.

You can register for online access through the KZN DOH portal. Visit portal.kznhealth.gov.za, navigate to the Online Library section, and follow the registration steps.

Yes, you may renew items once by calling the library before the due date. Further renewals are at the discretion of library staff depending on demand for the item.

Recommendations should be sent by email to the Library Manager from your Head of Department. The Library Manager will then include it in the acquisition list for approval.
