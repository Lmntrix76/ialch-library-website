About Our Library

IALCH Library, formerly known as Smith & Nephew Medical Library, was officially opened in July 2005. We provide access to the latest information in Health and Medicine to all IALCH staff.

Our growing collection includes over 2000 books and 200 journal subscriptions covering all fields in medicine and nursing.
