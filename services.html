Library Services

Supporting healthcare professionals with comprehensive resources

Acquisitions Procedure

  1. Collaborative Selection

    The selection/acquisition is a joint collaboration between the Clinical and Nursing Heads of Departments and the Library staff.

  2. Liaison Role

    The Library Manager acts as a liaison to each department, meeting periodically to discuss budget allocations and acquisition needs.

  3. Departmental Needs

    Selection is based on departmental or programme needs, with the Library staff ensuring balanced and fair acquisition.

  4. Budget Allocation

    Materials are acquired based on the budget allocated by the Head Office Library in Pietermaritzburg annually.

Circulation Services

Borrowing Privileges

All full-time IALCH staff may borrow up to four items. Materials must be returned or renewed by the due date.

Loan Periods

  • General Collection: 14 working days
  • Reference Materials: Library use only (overnight with permission)
  • Journals: Library use only

Renewals

Items may be renewed once unless requested by another patron. Call the library before the due date to renew.
